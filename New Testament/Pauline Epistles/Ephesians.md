### Chapter 2

Notes:

- From death in sin to life in Christ
- Unity of Jews and Gentiles in one body
- Christ broke down the dividing wall of hostility

Key Verses:
- Ephesians 2:4-5 - "But God, being rich in mercy, because of the great love with which he loved us, even when we were dead in our trespasses, made us alive together with Christ"

Notes:

- Paul's calling as apostle to the Gentiles
- The church displays God's wisdom to spiritual powers
- Prayer for spiritual strength and comprehension of Christ's love

Key Verses:

Summary:
Paul reveals the mystery that Gentiles are included in God's plan and prays that believers would comprehend the immeasurable love of Christ and be filled with God's fullness.

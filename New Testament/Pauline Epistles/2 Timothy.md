## Chapters

### Chapter 1
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Paul's thanksgiving for Timothy's sincere faith
- Encouragement to fan into flame the gift of God
- Call to not be ashamed of testimony about the Lord
- The gospel preserved through sound teaching

Key Verses:
- 2 Timothy 1:7 - "For God has not given us a spirit of fear, but of power and of love and of a sound mind"
- 2 Timothy 1:12 - "I know whom I have believed, and am convinced that he is able to guard what I have entrusted to him"
- 2 Timothy 1:13 - "Hold fast the pattern of sound words which you have heard from me"

Summary:
Paul encourages Timothy to remain bold in his faith and ministry, reminding him that God has given a spirit of power, not fear, and urging him to guard the gospel through the Holy Spirit.


### Chapter 2
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Call to be strong in grace and train faithful people
- Metaphors of soldier, athlete, and farmer for endurance
- Reminder to remember Jesus Christ risen from the dead
- Instructions to avoid quarrels and be a vessel for honor
- Call to flee youthful lusts and pursue righteousness

Key Verses:
- 2 Timothy 2:2 - "The things you have heard me say in the presence of many witnesses entrust to reliable people who will also be qualified to teach others"
- 2 Timothy 2:15 - "Study to show yourself approved to God, a worker who does not need to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth"
- 2 Timothy 2:22 - "Flee the evil desires of youth and pursue righteousness, faith, love and peace"

Summary:
Paul instructs Timothy to be a faithful teacher who trains others, endures hardship like a good soldier, and handles God's word correctly while pursuing godliness and avoiding foolish controversies.


### Chapter 3
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Warning about difficult times in the last days
- Description of godless characteristics of people
- Reminder of Timothy's background and training
- The inspiration and purpose of Scripture
- All Scripture is God-breathed and useful for teaching

Key Verses:
- 2 Timothy 3:12 - "Indeed, all who desire to live godly in Christ Jesus will be persecuted"
- 2 Timothy 3:15 - "From childhood you have known the Holy Scriptures, which are able to make you wise for salvation through faith in Christ Jesus"
- 2 Timothy 3:16-17 - "All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work"

Summary:
Paul warns Timothy about the perilous times ahead and encourages him to continue in what he has learned, emphasizing the divine inspiration and authority of Scripture for equipping believers.


### Chapter 4
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Solemn charge to preach the Word in all circumstances
- Warning that people will turn away from sound doctrine
- Paul's awareness of his approaching death
- Personal requests and final greetings
- Paul's confidence in his faithful completion of ministry

Key Verses:
- 2 Timothy 4:2 - "Preach the word; be prepared in season and out of season; correct, rebuke and encourage—with great patience and careful instruction"
- 2 Timothy 4:7-8 - "I have fought the good fight, I have finished the race, I have kept the faith. Now there is in store for me the crown of righteousness"
- 2 Timothy 4:17 - "But the Lord stood at my side and gave me strength, so that through me the message might be fully proclaimed"

Summary:
Paul gives Timothy his final charge to faithfully preach the gospel, shares his confidence in having completed his own ministry faithfully, and expresses his readiness for death while awaiting his heavenly reward.

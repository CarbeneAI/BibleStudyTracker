Key Verses:
- Romans 2:1 - "You, therefore, have no excuse, you who pass judgment on someone else"

### Chapter 3
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Universal sinfulness of all humanity
- No one is righteous, not even one
- Justification through faith in Jesus Christ
- God's righteousness revealed apart from the law

Key Verses:
- Romans 3:10 - "There is no one righteous, not even one"
- Romans 3:23 - "For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God"

Key Verses:

Paul uses Abraham as the prime example of justification by faith, showing that righteousness comes through believing God's promises, not through human effort.

- God's love demonstrated through Christ's death

Key Verses:
- Romans 5:1 - "Therefore, since we have been justified through faith, we have peace with God through our Lord Jesus Christ"

Paul explains the benefits of justification including peace with God and hope, and contrasts how sin entered through Adam while grace abounds through Christ.

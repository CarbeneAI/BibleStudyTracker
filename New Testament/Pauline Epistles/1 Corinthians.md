## Chapters

### Chapter 1
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Paul addresses divisions in the Corinthian church
- Emphasizes that the cross is central to the gospel
- God's wisdom versus worldly wisdom
- The church's unity should be in Christ alone

Key Verses:
- 1 Corinthians 1:10 - "I appeal to you, brothers and sisters, in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ, that all of you agree with one another"
- 1 Corinthians 1:18 - "For the message of the cross is foolishness to those who are perishing, but to us who are being saved it is the power of God"
- 1 Corinthians 1:27 - "But God chose the foolish things of the world to shame the wise"

Summary:
Paul confronts divisions in the church and reminds believers that the gospel message centers on Christ crucified, which appears foolish to the world but is God's power and wisdom.


### Chapter 2
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Paul's reliance on the Spirit's power, not eloquence
- Spiritual wisdom revealed by the Holy Spirit
- Natural person cannot understand spiritual truths
- Believers have the mind of Christ

Key Verses:
- 1 Corinthians 2:4-5 - "My message and my preaching were not with wise and persuasive words, but with a demonstration of the Spirit's power"
- 1 Corinthians 2:14 - "The person without the Spirit does not accept the things that come from the Spirit of God"
- 1 Corinthians 2:16 - "But we have the mind of Christ"

Summary:
Paul explains that true spiritual wisdom comes from the Holy Spirit, not human eloquence, and only those with the Spirit can understand God's truth.

Key Verses:

- Faith, hope, and love remain, but love is greatest

Key Verses:
- 1 Corinthians 13:4-7 - "Love is patient, love is kind. It does not envy, it does not boast, it is not proud..."

Paul's famous love chapter describes the supremacy and essential nature of love, showing that without love all spiritual gifts and accomplishments are meaningless.

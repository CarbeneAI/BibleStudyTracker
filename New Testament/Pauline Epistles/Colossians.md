## Chapters

### Chapter 1
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Paul's greeting and thanksgiving for the Colossian believers
- The supremacy of Christ in creation and redemption
- Christ is the image of the invisible God and holds all things together
- Paul's ministry to proclaim Christ and present everyone complete in Him

Key Verses:
- Colossians 1:15-16 - "He is the image of the invisible God, the firstborn over all creation. For by him all things were created"
- Colossians 1:18 - "He is the head of the body, the church; he is the beginning and the firstborn from among the dead"
- Colossians 1:27 - "Christ in you, the hope of glory"

Summary:
Paul establishes Christ's supremacy over all creation and His central role in redemption, emphasizing that in Him all things hold together and believers are reconciled to God.


### Chapter 2
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Warning against deceptive philosophy and hollow traditions
- Fullness of deity dwells in Christ bodily
- Believers are complete in Christ and freed from legalism
- Warning against worship of angels and harsh treatment of the body

Key Verses:
- Colossians 2:8 - "See to it that no one takes you captive through hollow and deceptive philosophy"
- Colossians 2:9-10 - "For in Christ all the fullness of the Deity lives in bodily form, and in Christ you have been brought to fullness"
- Colossians 2:14 - "Having canceled the charge of our legal indebtedness, which stood against us and condemned us; he has taken it away, nailing it to the cross"

Summary:
Paul warns the Colossians against false teachings and legalism, emphasizing that true spiritual fullness is found only in Christ, who has freed believers from religious regulations through His death on the cross.


### Chapter 3
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Put to death earthly nature and put on the new self in Christ
- Characteristics of the new life: compassion, kindness, humility, patience, forgiveness
- Instructions for Christian households: wives, husbands, children, fathers
- Work as unto the Lord in everything

Key Verses:
- Colossians 3:1-2 - "Set your minds on things above, not on earthly things"
- Colossians 3:12 - "Clothe yourselves with compassion, kindness, humility, gentleness and patience"
- Colossians 3:23 - "Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters"

Summary:
Paul instructs believers to live out their new identity in Christ by putting off sinful behaviors and putting on godly virtues, transforming all relationships and work into worship.


### Chapter 4
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Instructions for prayer and wise conduct toward outsiders
- Speech should be gracious, seasoned with salt
- Personal greetings and commendations of fellow workers
- Final instructions about reading and sharing the letter

Key Verses:
- Colossians 4:2 - "Devote yourselves to prayer, being watchful and thankful"
- Colossians 4:5-6 - "Be wise in the way you act toward outsiders; make the most of every opportunity. Let your conversation be always full of grace, seasoned with salt"

Summary:
Paul concludes with practical instructions for prayer, witness, and conduct, along with personal greetings to various co-workers in the gospel ministry.

### Chapter 1

Notes:

- Haggai calls Zerubbabel (governor) and Joshua (high priest) to lead temple rebuilding
- The remnant responds with obedience and begins construction work
- God promises His presence with them

Key Verses:
- Haggai 1:4 - "Is it a time for you yourselves to be living in your paneled houses, while this house remains a ruin?"
- Haggai 1:13 - "I am with you, declares the Lord"

Summary:
Haggai challenges the returned exiles to prioritize rebuilding God's temple, and the people respond with obedience under Zerubbabel and Joshua's leadership.

Notes:

Key Verses:

- Obadiah 1:21 - "And the kingdom will be the Lord's"

Summary:
Obadiah prophesies God's judgment against Edom for their pride and violence against Israel, declaring that the day of the Lord will bring justice to all nations and restoration to God's people.

## Chapters

### Chapter 1
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Habakkuk questions why God allows wickedness and injustice in Judah
- God responds that He will raise up the Babylonians as instruments of judgment
- The prophet struggles with how God can use such a wicked nation
- Themes of divine patience, judgment, and the problem of evil

Key Verses:
- Habakkuk 1:2 - "How long, Lord, must I call for help, but you do not listen?"
- Habakkuk 1:5 - "Look at the nations and watch—and be utterly amazed"
- Habakkuk 1:13 - "Why then do you tolerate the treacherous?"

Summary:
Habakkuk's complaint about injustice in Judah and God's surprising answer that He will use the Babylonians to bring judgment.


### Chapter 2
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Habakkuk waits on the watchtower for God's answer
- God declares five woes against the Babylonians for their pride and violence
- The famous declaration that "the righteous will live by faith"
- Assurance that God's glory will fill the earth despite present circumstances

Key Verses:
- Habakkuk 2:4 - "But the righteous person will live by his faithfulness"
- Habakkuk 2:14 - "For the earth will be filled with the knowledge of the glory of the Lord"
- Habakkuk 2:20 - "The Lord is in his holy temple; let all the earth be silent before him"

Summary:
God's response to Habakkuk affirming that the righteous will live by faith and that Babylon will face judgment for their wickedness.


### Chapter 3
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Habakkuk's prayer recounting God's mighty deeds in history
- Poetic description of God's power in creation and judgment
- Expression of faith despite dire circumstances
- Declaration of joy in God regardless of outward prosperity

Key Verses:
- Habakkuk 3:2 - "Lord, I have heard of your fame; I stand in awe of your deeds"
- Habakkuk 3:17-18 - "Though the fig tree does not bud... yet I will rejoice in the Lord"
- Habakkuk 3:19 - "The Sovereign Lord is my strength"

Summary:
Habakkuk's prayer of faith and worship, concluding with trust and joy in God even when facing hardship and uncertainty.

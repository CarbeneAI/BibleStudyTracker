### Chapter 1

Notes:
- God's love for Israel contrasted with rejection of Edom
- Priests offering defiled sacrifices and blemished animals
- God deserves honor and reverence, not contempt
- The Lord's name will be great among the nations

Key Verses:
- Malachi 1:2 - "I have loved you, says the Lord"
- Malachi 1:11 - "My name will be great among the nations"
- Malachi 1:6 - "A son honors his father, and a servant his master. If then I am a father, where is my honor?"

Summary:
God rebukes Israel for dishonoring Him with defiled sacrifices while declaring His love for them and His future glory among all nations.


### Chapter 2
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- Warning to the priests for breaking covenant with Levi
- Condemnation of divorce and marital unfaithfulness
- God hates divorce and covering one's garment with violence
- The people have wearied God with their words
- Messenger will prepare the way for the Lord

Key Verses:
- Malachi 2:16 - "The man who hates and divorces his wife, says the Lord, the God of Israel, does violence to the one he should protect"
- Malachi 2:7 - "For the lips of a priest ought to preserve knowledge"
- Malachi 2:10 - "Do we not all have one Father? Did not one God create us?"

Summary:
God rebukes the priests for corrupting the covenant and condemns the people for treachery in marriage and faithlessness to their covenant.


### Chapter 3
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- The messenger will prepare the way; the Lord will come to His temple
- God will refine and purify the Levites
- Condemnation of sorcerers, adulterers, those who oppress the weak
- Robbing God through withholding tithes and offerings
- Promise of blessing for those who bring tithes to the storehouse
- The arrogant vs. those who fear the Lord

Key Verses:
- Malachi 3:10 - "Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse... Test me in this, says the Lord Almighty"
- Malachi 3:1 - "I will send my messenger, who will prepare the way before me"
- Malachi 3:6 - "I the Lord do not change. So you, the descendants of Jacob, are not destroyed"

Summary:
God promises to send a messenger and refine His people, rebuking them for robbing Him through withheld tithes while inviting them to test His faithfulness.


### Chapter 4
- [ ] Read 📖 2025-MM-DD
Notes:
- The coming Day of the Lord will burn like a furnace
- The arrogant and evildoers will be stubble, but the righteous will be healed
- Sun of righteousness will rise with healing in its wings
- Remember the law of Moses
- Elijah will come before the great and dreadful day of the Lord
- He will turn hearts of parents to children and children to parents

Key Verses:
- Malachi 4:2 - "But for you who revere my name, the sun of righteousness will rise with healing in its rays"
- Malachi 4:5-6 - "See, I will send the prophet Elijah to you before that great and dreadful day of the Lord comes. He will turn the hearts of the parents to their children"
- Malachi 4:1 - "Surely the day is coming; it will burn like a furnace"

Summary:
The final chapter warns of judgment day for the wicked but promises healing for the righteous and prophesies Elijah's return to prepare hearts before the Lord comes.
